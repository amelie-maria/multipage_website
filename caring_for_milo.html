Taking Care of Your Dog

I have 2 year old spoodle named Milo

Keep your puppy active:

  • go on daily walks
  • play with your puppy (play catch, play tugawar, etc)
  • keep your puppy's mind engaged (teach/practice tricks, use ineractive feeders)

    • necessities:

  • treats for rewarding behavior
  • a dog bed
  • a collar, harness, and leash
  • dog food
  • grooming appointments
  • regular vet appointments!
